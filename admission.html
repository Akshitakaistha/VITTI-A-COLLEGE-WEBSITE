Admission Criteria

The candidate must have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination from a recognised Board or University with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory along with one of the Chemistry/BioTechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational Subjet securing atleast 45% marks in the above subjects together.

Basis of Admission

1.The admission to the first 50% of the seats shall be made on the basis of rank/merit/score obtained in Joint Entrance Exam (JEE MAIN).The reamining 50% seats will be filled up on the basis of merit of the Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Exam.

2.In case of seats remain vacant after exhausting the merit of JEE MAIN and HPCET, the remaining seats will be filled up on the basis of meri of 10+2 examination conducted by recognised Board or University.

3.The number of seats remain vacant to be filled on the basis of each test/examination will be shifted or filled on the basis of next examination in continuous order.
